<dt> </dt><h3>What is CS81?</h3>
<dd>
In CS81, we explore the mathematical foundations of computer science.  In the first
half of the course
consider propositional and predicate logic. 
We'll examine various proof techniques including natural deduction.  We
conclude this section of the course with Godel's Incompleteness
Theorems.
<p>
During the second half of the course we explore computability theory.  
We examine a variety of computatonal models in depth,
including finite automata,
pushdown autamata, and Turing machines.  We study the methods for proving a
problem solvable or unsolvable under each model.  We conclude with a review of 
other computational models including lambda calculus.
